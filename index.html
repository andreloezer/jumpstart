DeFrost: A Documentary

by Leo De Firenze

DeFrost is a documentary about growing up in a little red house in a cold harsh valley. This is a story about my mother's childhood. This film attempts to capture vignettes of her upbringing — her family, friends, pets, and eventual departure from a freezing valle in Northern Italy — through beatiful cinematography and narration.
